About me

Hello! I’m Abián, a junior front-end developer based in Las Palmas de Gran Canaria, Spain. I enjoy to work on exciting projects that test what I’ve learnt.

My interest in web development started back in 2014 when a friend ask me for a web app to his company. After that, I have developed some useful tools for the company i'm working.

I'm always looking for new challenges that can teach me new things or test my knowledges, but my main focus these days is to strengthen what I know about accessible and inclusive interfaces. Here are a few technologies I’ve been working with recently:

  • NextJs
